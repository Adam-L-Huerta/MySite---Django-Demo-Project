With God's inspired word as our guide, we strive to follow the New Testament pattern for conversion, worship and Christian life. We want to be simply Christian in the things we do in service to the Lord. We seek to study God's word diligently and practice what we find in its pages so that those who are part of our Christian family here are devoted to PRAISING our God, PARTICIPATING in the work of the church on earth, and PROCLAIMING the truth of the Gospel and the excellencies of our Father to the world.

Our Mission

We strive bring glory to God by the equipping of the saints for the work of service, to the building up of the body of Christ; until we all attain to the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God (Ephesians 4:12-13).

Our Values

With prayerful recognition of the authority and sovereignty of the Almighty God, we will always endeavor to:

Bring glory and praise to God.
Save souls and keep them saved.
Be biblically balanced, scripturally sound and Christ-centered.
Edify one another as a spiritual family.
Treat all people with love, compassion and respect.
